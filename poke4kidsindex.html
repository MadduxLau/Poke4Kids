Who We Are
Our Mission

"To help all kids enjoy the Pokémon TCG hobby and not let financial restraints limit their happiness."

Pokémon cards have brought joy, friendship, and endless hours of fun to millions of kids around the world — but for many children in New York City's public schools, the cost of getting started puts that joy just out of reach.

Poke4Kids was founded on a simple belief: every kid deserves a chance to play. We bridge the gap between collectors who have more cards than they need and kids who have never held a single one. Your bulk donations — the commons, the duplicates, the forgotten binders in your closet — become someone's very first collection.

We are a community-driven initiative run entirely by volunteers who grew up loving Pokémon and want to pass that love on to the next generation of trainers across the five boroughs.
